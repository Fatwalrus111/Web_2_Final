Fred is an avid lego collector and builder. He has many lego sets, primarily lego city and lego star wars. His favorite minifigs are Captain Rex, Commander Cody, and an OG Arc trooped from a battle pack. His favorite starwars set is either the clone gunship or ATTE.
This isn't a comprehensive list of his hobbies, but Fred is just a chill guy who is a lot nerdier then people normally stereotype him as being.
