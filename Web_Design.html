Why Fred Codes
While he might still be a novice, Fred enjoys the problem solving and creative aspects of coding. Each new goal brings with it new challenges, but also new solutions. To Fred coding feels less like a math problem, where there is one right way to get the right solution, and more like a free flowing project, like a wall of rock that you need to climb.
