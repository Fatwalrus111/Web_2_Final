Hi, I’m Fred Coddon Rooted in Ballard, Washington, and raised on spirited discussion, I’ve spent the last few years developing my voice — in classrooms, courtrooms (mock ones, for now), and across the debate circuit. I’m the Washington State Public Forum Debate Champion, a two-time national qualifier, and a passionate student of law, rhetoric, and the power of ideas.

This site is a hub for my work, my story, and what’s next. Whether you're here to learn about my academic journey, check out my achievements, or get in touch, I’m glad you’re here.
